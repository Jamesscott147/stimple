What is Stimple?

Stimple is an ebook created with the emerging artist in mind. What this handbook aims to teach, is the art of stipple shading in the most simple way possible focusing on the materials, tools, some simple exercises and more.

What will stimple teach me?

Tools

Save time digging through forums and video comments trying to find what particular pen or paper is the best. Stimple provides all this information.

Exercises

Get started right away with some easy and basic exercises that will help you understand the fundamentals of stipple shading.

Knowledge

Get a quick history lesson on the origins of stippling as well as some insight as to where the art form is at today.
